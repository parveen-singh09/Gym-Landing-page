COMMON INQUIRIES

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Can I try out the gym for free before committing?

Yes! We offer a one-day trial pass for local residents. You can register for your trial pass via the contact form, and our guest services team will set up your access card.

Are there any registration fees or hidden lock-in contracts?

Absolutely not. We believe in total pricing honesty. All memberships are billed on a month-to-month basis (or annually if you opt for the discounted package), and you can freeze or cancel at any time with a 10-day notice.

What are the opening hours for members?

Our Pro Athlete and Elite Squad members have full 24/7 keyless app access. Basic members can access the gym facility during standard hours: Monday through Friday from 5:00 AM to 11:00 PM, and Saturday/Sunday from 7:00 AM to 8:00 PM.
